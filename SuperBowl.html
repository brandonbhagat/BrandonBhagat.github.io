Seattle Seahawks

The Seahawks finished the regular season 12-4 led by QB Russell Wilson and RB Marshawn Lynch. They beat the Baltimore Ravens and the Green Bay Packers in the the 2015 NFL Playoffs and will take on New England in Arizona for the Super Bowl.

New England Patriots

The Patriots finished the regular season 12-4 led by QB Tom Brady and TE Rob Gronkowski. They beat the Baltimore Ravens and the Indianapolis Colts in the 2015 NFL Playoffs and will take on Seattle in Arizona for the Super Bowl.
