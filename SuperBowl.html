Teams

 Represents KAM's picks

Green Bay Packers

The Packers finished the regular season 12-4 led by QB Aaron Rodgers and RB Eddie Lacy. They beat the Dallas Cowboys in the Divisional Playoffs and will travel to Seattle for the Conference Playoffs.

Seattle Seahawks

The Seahawks finished the regular season 12-4 led by QB Russell Wilson and their vaunted defense. They beat the Carolina Panthers in the Divisional Playoffs and will take on Green Bay at home in the Conference Playoffs.

New England Patriots

The Patriots finished the regular season 12-4 led by QB Tom Brady and TE Rob Gronkowski. They beat the Baltimore Ravens in the Divisional Playoffs and will take on Indianapolis at home in the Conference Playoffs.

Indianapolis Colts

The Colts finished the regular season 11-5 led by QB Andrew Luck and WR T.Y. Hilton. They beat the Denver Broncos in the Divisional Playoffs and will take on New England in the Conference Playoffs.
